ABOUT US

The Cavite State University Imus Campus Ticketing and Booking System is an online platform designed for
students to reserve tickets for school activities, including events, performances, etc.
It offers a fast and convenient way to secure slots and receive digital tickets.

This system helps make student participation more organized, efficient, and accessible across the campus.
